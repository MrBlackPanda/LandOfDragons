About Us

Our Mission

Dragons cannot be truly "tamed", but they can be bonded with and trained. The Valyrians rode dragons for millennia. Aegon I Targaryen and his sister-wives, who descended from Old Valyria, used the last three Mega dragons in the world to conquer and unify the Seven Kingdoms. We want to instill the confidence and competence in you to be able to make that bond possible.
